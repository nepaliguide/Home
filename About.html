Empowering Every Aspirant.

Nepali Guide is Nepal's premier digital destination for Loksewa and PSC preparation, built to make quality education accessible to all.

Our Journey

Preparing for the Public Service Commission (PSC) in Nepal can be a challenging journey. Our platform was created with a single goal: to provide a centralized, modern hub for study materials that are often hard to find or outdated.

From General Knowledge (GK) to complex IQ reasoning, we simplify the syllabus for exams like Kharidar, Nasu, and Officer, as well as banking sectors like NRB, RBB, and NBL.
