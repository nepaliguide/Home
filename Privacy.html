Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024. Your privacy is our top priority.

Introduction

Welcome to Nepali Guide. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website and use our Loksewa preparation tools.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information in two ways:

  • Personal Data: When you use our contact form or register, we collect your name and email address.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information like IP address, browser type, and pages visited to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:
• Provide and maintain our Loksewa Mock Tests.
• Respond to your inquiries via our contact forms.
• Send periodic updates regarding new study materials.
• Monitor website traffic to enhance user experience.

3. Cookies and Advertising

Nepali Guide uses "Cookies" to enhance your experience. We may show advertisements through third-party vendors like Google AdSense. These vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (like SSL encryption) to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
